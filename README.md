# Skills

- [**post-architect**](post-architect) — Helps you find the structure of a blog post or article before you write it, through a short interview that draws out your own ideas and arranges them into a speakable scaffold.
- [**tidy-transcription**](tidy-transcription) — Cleans up speech-to-text transcripts into readable prose while keeping your words and voice intact. Drops filler words, fixes grammar, and repairs transcription errors without rewriting what you said.
